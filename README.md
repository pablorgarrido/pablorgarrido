## About Me

Senior Software Engineer with **19+ years** of experience designing and delivering **mission-critical, high-availability systems** at national scale. Currently at **Fundação Cesgranrio**, where I serve as a technical reference for Brazil's most high-stakes digital platforms — including **ENEM Digital** (Brazil's national university entrance exam) and the **CPNU** (National Unified Public Service Examination).

My career spans the full spectrum: from large-enterprise consulting at **Accenture** (Sicredi, Santander, Embratel) and the financial sector (Banco do Brasil, Caixa, ANBIMA), to freelance work in **Sydney, Australia**. I bring a systems-thinking mindset to every layer of the stack — architecture, performance engineering, legacy modernization, and team leadership.

- Designing for **millions of concurrent users** — performance is a feature, not an afterthought
- Strong background in **Clean Architecture**, **SOLID**, **Microservices** and **SOA**
- Active role in **ISO 27001** certification processes and secure software practices (LGPD)
- Technical mentor, workshop facilitator, and code standards architect for engineering teams
- **MBA in Software Engineering — UFRJ** | Upper-Intermediate English | International experience
